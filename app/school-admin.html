Amudim looks beyond the “what” to explore the “why’s” of Jewish texts.
Edit

Amudim is a year in Israel program exclusively for young Anglo women to approach Torah learning from traditional and academic perspectives.

Amudim is the new kind of Shana Ba-Aretz experience that today’s global and information-packed age demands. Not your average seminary, Amudim offers a modern approach to Torah learning, courses and extracurricular opportunities you won’t find anywhere else, all within a warm environment that stresses Shmirat ha-Mitzvot. Amudim stands at the forefront of advanced Torah education, addressing the needs of curious and motivated young women seeking an intellectual and experiential year in Israel. Amudim provides a unique framework for understanding, appreciating and finding oneself in the Jewish world today.

With the stunning, modern and diverse city of Modiin as its backdrop, Amudim is a year in Israel program exclusively for Anglo women to engage with Jewish texts from both traditional and academic perspectives. in our Beit Midrash, no question or source is off the table and students are encouraged to think on their own.
